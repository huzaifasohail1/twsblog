The Power of Education: A Personal Reflection on the Impact of Teachers

Huzaifa Sohail

Education is an essential part of life that can have a lasting impact on our future. As SD Brookfield writes in his 2017 book, "Teachers are the most powerful agents of meaningful change in our students' lives." My teachers pushed me to think critically, form my own thoughts, values, and beliefs. They have also assisted me in developing as a person and discovering my potential. I am especially grateful for the access I have had to quality education, both in my hometown and abroad, which has enabled me to gain a more global perspective on the world. Through my educational journey, I have come to understand the power of knowledge and the importance of striving to become a lifelong learner. As we celebrate Fifth International Day of Education, I want to take a moment to express my deepest gratitude to the educators in my life. They have played an instrumental role in shaping who I am today. I especially want to express my gratitude to my CS teacher, Ms. Anuranji, and my career counsellor Ms. Lali for providing me with numerous opportunities. Thank you for your guidance, mentorship, and passion for teaching.
